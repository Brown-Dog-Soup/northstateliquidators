The process

How a pallet gets from Amazon to you.

01

Truck Arrives

We buy truckloads direct from retail return centers. 15–25 pallets per load. Two loads a week, typically.

02

We Sort & Grade

Every pallet gets broken down, cataloged, photographed. Condition tags: New, Shelf Pull, Customer Return, Untested.

03

We List Online

Pallets go up here with full manifests. Retail-grade items head to the retail floor and the "Hunt" listing.

04

You Buy

Pickup at the Raleigh warehouse, or we freight it. Resellers can set up a standing weekly order.
