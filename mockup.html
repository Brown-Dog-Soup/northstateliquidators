The story —

Two guys, a truck, and a loading dock in Raleigh.

We started North State because we got tired of watching perfectly good merchandise get ground up in the return stream. Amazon, Lowe's, Target — they can't put an open-box item back on the shelf. So it ends up in a return center, and from there it's headed to a landfill unless somebody buys the pallet.

That's where we come in. We drive the truck, we unload the pallets, we sort every unit by hand. Brand-new gets one bin; open-box-tested gets another; untested-return gets another. Then it comes out here on the floor, or it goes back out on a pallet to a reseller. No middle layer. No mystery.

If you're a shopper, come walk the floor. If you're a reseller, call the number at the top. We pick up. That's a promise most liquidators won't make.

— Norm & Rob
