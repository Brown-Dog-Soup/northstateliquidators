Engagement Overview

TenantIQ Pro LLC will stand up the three core pieces of North State Liquidators' digital operation: branded business email on @northstateliquidators.com, a Shopify storefront with a phone-app workflow for listing inventory from the warehouse floor, and continued hosting of the public marketing website at northstateliquidators.com. Mailboxes are hosted as a guest domain inside TenantIQ Pro's Microsoft 365 tenant — NSL gets the full business-grade experience without having to operate its own tenant.

Friends & Family Engagement — all TenantIQ Pro labor (setup, DNS, onboarding, training, and ongoing administration) is provided at $0. NSL pays only the true third-party pass-through costs (Microsoft, Shopify, GoDaddy) at cost with no markup. This proposal exists to document scope, ownership, and terms in writing — not to bill time.

Terms & Conditions
Engagement Term Month-to-month. No minimum commitment. Either party may terminate with thirty (30) days' written notice (email is fine). No termination penalty.
Billing TenantIQ Pro LLC invoices NSL monthly (or quarterly, at NSL's option) for pass-through subscriptions at cost. Net-15. Annual domain renewal invoiced in the month GoDaddy charges, or paid directly by NSL. No service fee, no markup, no minimum charge.
Tenant Ownership & Admin The Microsoft 365 tenant is owned and administered by TenantIQ Pro LLC under the domain tenantiqpro.com. NSL's mailboxes and data reside within that tenant as a verified custom domain (northstateliquidators.com). TenantIQ Pro holds Global Administrator rights and will use them only for authorized troubleshooting, license management, and requested changes. A dedicated delegated-admin account (admin@northstateliquidators.com) can be provisioned for NSL on request.
Data Ownership All mailbox contents, calendars, contacts, Shopify product data, order data, customer data, and website content remain the sole property of North State Liquidators, LLC. NSL may request a full export at any time in standard formats (PST for mailboxes, CSV for Shopify).
Privacy TenantIQ Pro will not read, copy, forward, or analyze NSL mailbox contents or customer data. Admin access will be used only for legitimate operational tasks (license assignment, delivery troubleshooting, mailbox restore). A mutual non-disclosure applies to any NSL business information observed incidentally during support.
Exit & Migration If NSL chooses to move to its own Microsoft 365 tenant or a different provider, TenantIQ Pro will assist with migration at $0 labor (friends rate). NSL pays only destination-tenant license costs and any third-party migration tooling. TenantIQ Pro will release the custom domain promptly on request.
Third-Party Agreements NSL's use of Microsoft 365, Shopify, GoDaddy, and GitHub is governed by each vendor's own Terms of Service. TenantIQ Pro is not a party to those agreements and is not liable for vendor outages, price changes, or policy changes.
Rate & Scope Changes Pass-through costs follow whatever Microsoft, Shopify, or GoDaddy charge. If a vendor raises its price, the new rate is passed through with at least 30 days' written notice. Any change from friends-rate ($0 labor) to paid services requires a new written agreement signed by both parties before work begins.
Support Best-effort support by phone, text, or email. No SLA. Response typically same-day on business days. Emergencies (total mail outage, site down) handled as a priority.
Limitation of Liability TenantIQ Pro LLC's aggregate liability under this engagement shall not exceed the total pass-through fees paid in the three (3) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
